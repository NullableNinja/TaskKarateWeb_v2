FAQ

Student Handbook

What age are the classes?
Kids class is typically ages 6–11, and the Teen/Adult class is 12+. Truly—you’re never too old to start.
How often should I attend?
Plan on at least 2–3 times per week for steady progress.
Do kids use karate in the wrong way?
We emphasize respect, self-control, and never abusing martial arts skills.
How long to achieve Black Belt?
Minimum is about 3½ years, and often longer—consistent training is key.
