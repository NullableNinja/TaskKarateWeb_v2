Leadership Pipeline

How assistants grow their skills

Assistants are dedicated students who train with Mr. Thomson, earn trust, and learn how to coach with the same positive energy they receive. Many start by helping newer students, then take on more responsibility as their own technique and teaching skills advance.

  • Earned spots: Assistants are invited based on consistent attendance, great etiquette, and a willingness to help.
  • Ongoing mentorship: They shadow Mr. Thomson, learning how to cue techniques, manage groups, and keep lessons moving.
  • Community focus: Helpers make the dojo welcoming for newcomers and seasoned students alike.
